The product of all positive real values of x satisfying the equation x(16(log5x)3−68log5x)=5-16 is
JEE Advanced 2022 (Paper 2)

Solution:

Taking log to the base 5 on both sides
(16(log5x)3−68(log5x))(log5x)=−16
Let (log5x)=t
16t4−68t2+16=0
⇒4t4−16t2−t2+4=0
⇒(4t2−1)(t2−4)=0
⇒t=±1/2,±2
So log5x=±1/2 or ±2
⇒x=51/2, 5-1/2, 52, 5-2
Hence, the product is 5–√×15√×25×125=1
